Eligibility Criteria

  • Overall health- The donor must be fit and healthy, and should not be suffering from transmittable diseases.
  • Age and weight- The donor must be 18–65 years old and should weigh a minimum of 50 kg.
  • Pulse rate- Between 50 and 100 without irregularities.
  • Hemoglobin level- A minimum of 12.5 g/dL.
  • Blood pressure- Diastolic: 50–100 mm Hg, Systolic: 100–180 mm Hg.
  • Body temperature- Should be normal, with an oral temperature not exceeding 37.5 °C.
  • The time period between successive blood donations should be more than 3 months.
  • You should not be suffering from any of the following diseases or taking medicines for them
        → Hepatitis B, C/ AIDS/ Cancer/ Hemophilia/ Bleeding problems/ Hormonal disorders
        → Fits/ Convulsions/Diabetes (are you under medication currently?)
        → Any allergies (Only if you are suffering from severe symptoms)
        → Kidney/ Heart disease
        → Leprosy or any other infectious diseases
        → Tuberculosis (within 2 years)
        → Chicken Pox/ Malaria (within 1 year)
        → Organ Transplant (within one year)
        → Blood Transfusion/ Pregnancy (within the last 6 months)
        → Blood Donation/ Major Surgery (within the last 3 months)
        → Small Pox Vaccination (within the last 3weeks)
        → Hemoglobin deficiency / Anemia/ Drastic weight loss (recently)
